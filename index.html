Why It Matters

Unchecked water hyacinth growth devastates aquatic life.

Oxygen Depletion

Thick mats block air-water interface, lowering Dissolved Oxygen (DO) levels and suffocating fish.

Sunlight Blockage

Prevents sunlight from reaching underwater plants, halting photosynthesis and crashing the food web.

Disease Vector

Stagnant water trapped by weeds becomes a breeding ground for mosquitoes and other disease carriers.

Biodiversity Loss

Native species are displaced as the invasive weed mono-culture takes over the entire water body.
